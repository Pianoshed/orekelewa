📜 Ewi Ife (Love Poems)

Orekelewa Mi
Orekelewa mi, arewa to ju arewa lo
Oju re dun bi oyin, eti re dara bi fadaka
Bi mo ba wo o, okan mi a ma jo
Ife mi fun e ko ni le tan lai lai

O joko soke bi osumare l'oke
Ewa re ko l'apeere laye yi
Bi osupa ti n tan l'oru
Be e ni iwo n tan ninu okan mi

Translation:
My beautiful one, beauty beyond compare
Your eyes sweet as honey, your ears precious as silver
When I see you, my heart dances
My love for you will never end
Ayanfe Mi
Ayanfe mi, o ti gba okan mi
Ife to wa laarin wa, o dara pupo
Bi omi ti n san l'odo
Be e ni ife mi n san fun e

Iwo nikan l'emi fe
Iwo nikan ni mo mo
L'aiye at'orun, iwo l'emi yoo ma fe
Titi aye ti orun

Translation:
My chosen one, you've captured my heart
The love between us is so beautiful
Like water flowing in a stream
So does my love flow for you
Omoge Adun
Omoge adun, oro mi o le tan
Nitori pe, ife to wa ninu okan mi fun e
Ko ni tan rara
Bi oorun ti n yo l'owuro
Be e ni oju mi n yo nitori re

Ife wa yoo duro titi lai
Ko si ohun to le ya wa
Emi at'iwo, iwo at'emi
La o ma wa titi aye

Translation:
Sweet beautiful one, my words cannot end
Because the love in my heart for you
Will never finish
As the sun rises in the morning
So does my face brighten because of you
